A bit more about how I work

I started out curious about how websites actually worked under the hood, and that curiosity turned into building full projects — a quiz app, an AI chat clone, a country explorer — each one picked because it forced me to learn something I didn't know yet, whether that was auth flows, real-time APIs, or just structuring a database properly.

I care about the small details: clean component structure, a UI that feels obvious to use, and code I'd be comfortable handing to someone else. Outside of coursework, I spend a fair bit of time on LeetCode and GeeksforGeeks, partly for interview prep and partly because I genuinely enjoy a good problem.
